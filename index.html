/Doh-gu-ber/

Dog walking is the act of a person walking with a dog, typically from the dog's residence and then returning. This constitutes part of the daily exercise regime needed to keep a dog healthy.

What is Doguber?

Many people find it increasingly difficult to take time out of their day to walk their dogs. Personal dog walkers are expensive while hired dog walkers might steal the dog. Furthermore, dog owners are unable to track the dog’s whereabouts due to the lack of trust with the dog walker.

Why Doguber?

Many people in Singapore are becoming dog owners and pet-care market in Singapore is hence growing rapidly. However, many of the dog owners do not have the time to walk their dogs due to Singapore’s fast-paced lifestyle. Our product helps to solve this common problem many dog owners or aspiring dog owners face - so, start now!

How Doguber?

  1. Dog owners send out a request into the website for people to walk their dogs
  2. The requests would then be collated onto our website
  3. The dog walkers are able to review the requests and then accept the requests
  4. The dog walkers will go to the house to collect the dog to walk it at the collection timing indicated by the dog owner.
